The through‑line

"What every Yarbo video shows"

  • Hot-swap process — chassis stays, module locks in

  • The Yarbo app — scheduling, geo-fence, remote start, real-time status on Carin's phone
  • Safety system — obstacle detection, lift sensor, rain pause

  • Battery + runtime — single charge, multi-zone coverage

"What changes from one video to the next"

  • Summer — Mower video: blade configuration, cut quality, slope handling
  • Summer — Trimmer video (package 2 & 3): edge precision on borders, beds, hard-to-reach

Two Directions

Same coverage, different feel.

Whichever direction you pick, every video still covers the through-line plus its season-specific focus. The choice is about tone, not content.

Direction A

Authentic UGC

"Carin's voice, real garden time."

Filmed in Carin's natural voice. Handheld, conversational commentary ("here's what surprised me"). High-trust format — the @villasjovik audience responds to Carin like a neighbor, not an actor. Modern social-native feel for brands building organic credibility.

UGC · Handheld · Conversational

Direction B

Cinematic product story

"Polished motion, flagship-feel."

Stabilized motion, macro detail shots of the platform in action, color graded post, voiceover narration over polished cuts. Production-grade asset that runs as Meta ad creative as naturally as it sits in organic feed. Strong for brands wanting a flagship product anthem.

Cinematic · Macro · Voiceover
